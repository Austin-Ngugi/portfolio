Flood quality and Water Quality Monitoring System

I am the project lead for the Flood Detector and Water Quality Monitoring System, an innovative solution designed to monitor rising water levels and detect pollution through Water conductivity, PH and water temperature measurements. The system uses to measure water levels, helping to track river conditions and prevent potential disasters, while the water quality sensors assess water quality for pollution detection. The collected data is transmitted in real-time to a web-based dashboard for monitoring and early warnings. I was primarily responsible for the mechanical and electrical design of the system and also played a key role in coordinating the team working on the dashboard and cloud integration.
